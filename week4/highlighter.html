Milestones toward discovering gifts and developing talents:

Take a moment to be grateful for the gifts you know you have. Your life, your health, good friends, good family, whatever it is.

Reflect on the things you have valued in your experience. Was there anything you did well at that you haven't done in a while? Was there some interest you had, like art or music, that you haven't tried yet?

Discover your talents, then work harder, smarter, and faster to grow them.

Work harder at your talent by establishing a daily practice or regular discipline.

Work smarter and faster at your talent by finding role models and a mentor.

Don't compare yourself to ANYONE except the person that you were yesterday.
